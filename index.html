About the Game

Fast-paced space survival

Neon Dodger: Space Run challenges players to survive longer, react faster and master an increasingly intense neon obstacle field. The game combines arcade movement, sector progression, rewards and unlockable ships with a clean futuristic presentation.

What players can do

  • Dodge neon blocks and survive as long as possible.
  • Collect coins during runs and spend them in the shop.
  • Unlock ships with different speed profiles.
  • Claim daily rewards and keep progressing.
  • View cards, missions and map sectors from the main interface.

Why it stands out

The game uses a consistent neon palette, arcade-style typography, polished menus and focused controls. It is designed to feel modern, clean and responsive while keeping the gameplay easy to learn and hard to master.
