Privacy Policy

Neon Dodger: Space Run

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Novanolo Apps handles information related to the mobile game Neon Dodger: Space Run. The app is designed as an arcade game experience and does not require account registration to play.

2. Information the app may use

  • Local gameplay data: the app may store progress such as scores, coins, unlocked ships, reward timing and settings on your device.
  • Contact information: if you email us for support, we will receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address and the details of your request.

3. How information is used

  • To save and restore gameplay progress and settings.
  • To respond to support questions or requests.
  • To improve the quality and stability of the game.

4. Data sharing

Novanolo Apps does not sell personal information. If third-party services such as analytics, advertising or cloud features are added in future versions, this policy should be updated to reflect those services and how they process user data.

5. Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app. If you believe that personal information has been provided to us in error, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

6. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect information that is stored locally or sent to us through direct communication. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we aim to handle support and gameplay data responsibly.
